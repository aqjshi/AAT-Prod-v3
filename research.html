Innovation Program

American Advanced Technology accepts proposals under the Innovation Program. The purpose of the Innovation Program is to support research and development (R&D) activities that bring advancement in science, technology, engineering and mathematics with applications in the digital transformation, transportation, energy, water, foodstuff granary, construction, environment, finance, safety, health and education sectors. Depending on the sector, there are different research proposal templates to be used. Researchers are encouraged to email customer_service@usaaat.com for a research proposal template in a specific sector or for inquiries.

Indirect Cost Policy. The indirect cost rate proposed in the budget of a grant submitted to American Advanced Technology must be 0% of Modified Total Direct Costs (MTDC). Indirect cost rate, also known as Facilities & Administrative (F&A) rate, is defined in NIH Grants Policy Statement.

Entrepreneurship Program

American Advanced Technology accepts proposals under the Entrepreneurship Program. The purpose of the Entrepreneurship Program is to nurture entrepreneurs (only small-sized enterprises), adding value to their existing operations and assisting regional economic development. The Program not only provides seed funding to support startup companies, but also provides technical, marketing, international trade, budgetary, financial, legal, human resource and managerial expertise as well as other resources to facilitate the buildup of an ecosystem vital to the success of the start-up companies.
