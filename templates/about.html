Who We Are

Established in 2003 under the name of American Advanced SUN Technologies, Inc., the company was renamed as American Advanced Technology, LLC in 2010. Located in Potomac, Maryland, American Advanced Technology is a minority owned small business enterprise (MBE/SBE/DBE undergoing certification) providing our customers with engineering and technology consulting, services and products in transportation, water and wastewater, energy, environmental and public health, and safety, smart city and infrastructure, software, information technology, artificial intelligence and financial sectors.
