Methane Detector System

Methane Detection System is used to detect methane gas leaks and remotely monitor changes in the methane concentration at project and network levels. Individual calibration and testing for measurements. Extended temperature range (-40 °C to +60 °C), internal microprocessor for advanced signal processing, GSM (4G) or WiFi (2.4 GHz) digital communication, GPS localization, rugged aluminum enclosure (EN AB46100), optional distant pellistor output, and IR‑long optical path for better resolution.

Air Quality Monitoring System

Air Quality Monitoring System is used to monitor air quality at project and network levels. Functionality includes detecting and localizing (GPS) gas leaks, measuring gas concentrations (CO₂, NO₂, SO₂, CO, C₂H₂, C₂H₄), humidity, and temperature; data preprocessing and sharing via GSM, LAN, or WiFi; a dashboard with visual and audible alarms when concentrations exceed predefined thresholds; and air quality control for particulate matter (PM1.0, PM2.5, PM4, PM10), VOC, and NOₓ indexing.

Bridge Monitoring System

Bridge Monitoring System is used to monitor bridge health, safety, and traffic conditions at project and network levels. Functionality includes measuring displacement, vibration, strain, cracking, humidity, temperature, concrete spalling, using optical and wireless communication for data transmission, and providing a dashboard with visual and audible alarms when measurements exceed predefined thresholds.
