Training

American Advanced Technology empowers people through education, training and workforce development activities. Download and fill out the Training Inquiry Form and email it to customer_service@usaaat.com if you are interested in one of the following programs. Our customer service will reach out to you for a no-cost consultation before you decide to register in a Program.

  • Summer and Winter Camp Program for K-12 and College Students
  • Culture, Art and Design Program for K-12, Community College and College Students
  • College Access Counseling Program for High School Students
  • Certificate and Internship Program for High School, Trade School, Community College and College Students
  • Incumbent Training Program for Working Professionals and Community Residents
