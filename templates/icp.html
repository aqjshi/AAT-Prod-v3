Indirect Cost Policy

The indirect cost rate, also known as Facilities & Administrative (F&A) rate, used in a grant budget submitted to American Advanced Technology should not exceed 0% of Modified Total Direct Costs (MTDC) defined in the Code of Federal Regulations.

Effective Date

This Notice is effective as of May 24, 2025.
