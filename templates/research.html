Research

American Advanced Technology and its partners conduct collaborative activities under Innovation Program and Entrepreneurship Program in the following areas to enhance the planning, design, construction, operations, safety, maintenance and management of cities and infrastructure, to optimize financial risk, and to leverage people’s health and safety benefits. Publications are accessible from Google Scholar.

Research & Development Program

The purpose of the Research & Development Program is to support scientific research and development (R&D) activities that bring innovation and advancement in science, technology, engineering, art and mathematics (STEAM) in the digital transformation, transportation, energy, water, foodstuff granary, construction, environment, finance, health, safety and education sectors. American Advanced Technology accepts R&D proposals under the Innovation Program on a rolling basis. Researchers are encouraged to contact AAT customer service for inquiry prior to proposal submission or submit a proposal via email to  customer_service@usaaat.com with a subject line title of “Proposal Submission”.

Entrepreneurship Program

Interested parties are encouraged to contact AAT customer service for inquiry prior to proposal submission or submit a proposal via email to customer_service@usaaat.com  with a subject “Proposal Submission”.
