Notice of Nondiscrimination

The American Advanced Technology does not discriminate on the basis of race, color, national origin, age, or handicap in a program or activity receiving EPA assistance or, in programs or activities covered by section 13, on the basis of sex. The President is designated as the civil rights coordinator.

Effective Date

This Notice is effective as of May 24, 2025.
